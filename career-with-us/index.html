    Join Our Team at Ananda Vriddhi Foundation

    Career With Us

    The Ananda Vriddhi Foundation is implementing the "Dekho Apna Desh Program," which aims to provide Virtual Reality (VR) tours across Old Age Homes, Assisted Living Facilities for Elderly, and Children living in Shelters and Orphanages across various Cities and Towns in India with support from individual donors & MS TellMe Digiinfotech Pvt. Ltd. This initiative seeks to enhance mental well-being, evoke joyful memories, and provide immersive experiences of India's attractions and nature through advanced VR technology. We seek support from volunteers to facilitate these programs and contribute toward this noble cause.
    We invite you to enlist yourself in our mission! As a volunteer, you'll work alongside our team to guide delegates on virtual tours of India's stunning attractions using advanced VR headsets. This is a fantastic opportunity to enhance your CV while gaining hands-on experience in an innovative field!

    Volunteers Opportunities

    We offer:

    • An opportunity to engage with the community targeting mental well-being.
    • Experience with cutting-edge technology in Virtual Reality.
    • Certification from the Foundation.

    This program not only aims at enhancing the quality of life for participants but also provides a meaningful avenue for volunteers to contribute positively while gaining valuable experience in a growing field

    Objectives:

    • Enhance Mental Well-Being: Foster joy and happiness among the elderly and children by providing them with virtual experiences that they may not have the opportunity to enjoy in person.
    • Memory Stimulation: Help elderly individuals relive cherished memories and create new experiences.
    • Community Engagement: Involve volunteers who are passionate about serving the community and making a difference.

    Volunteer Roles and Responsibilities

    Volunteers will be integral to the success of the program. Their roles will include:

    • Facilitating VR Tours: Guiding participants through virtual experiences, ensuring they are engaged and comfortable.
    • Technical Support: Assisting with the setup and operation of VR equipment.
    • Emotional Support: Providing companionship and encouragement to participants during the tours.

    Benefits for Volunteers

    Volunteers will receive:

    • Community Engagement: An opportunity to contribute positively to the lives of others.
    • Experience in Innovative Technology: Hands-on experience with VR technology, enhancing their skill set.
    • Certification: A certificate from Ananda Vriddhi Foundation acknowledging their contribution.

    Internship Opportunities

    Scope of Work

    PR and Communication:As an intern in PR and Communication, you will play a key role in promoting AVF's work through multiple communication channels. Your responsibilities will include:

    • Creating engaging social media content.
    • Drafting and distributing press releases.
    • Building and maintaining relationships with media outlets.
    • Tracking and analyzing the success of PR efforts.

    Dekho Apna Desh Program:Join us in organizing the Dekho Apna Desh program, aimed at educating children and the elderly about India's rich heritage and culture. Your tasks will involve:

    • Coordinating with schools, institutions, and elderly homes.
    • Developing educational materials.
    • Collecting and analyzing feedback on the program's impact.

    Stakeholder Coordination:In this role, you will liaise with NGOs and other partners to collaborate on development initiatives. Your duties will include:

    • Pitching programs and conducting demonstration drives.
    • Attending and contributing to meetings.
    • Ensuring the timely implementation of joint projects.

    Documentation and Reporting:Help us keep track of our progress and achievements. As part of your responsibilities, you will:

    • Document the foundation's activities and events.
    • Take meeting minutes and compile data.
    • Prepare comprehensive reports.

    Learning and Development:We believe in continuous learning and development. As an intern, you will have the opportunity to:

    • Attend training sessions and workshops.
    • Learn about new approaches in the social sector.
    • Contribute ideas for new initiatives and programs.

    Why Join AVF?

    • Impact: Contribute to meaningful projects that make a real difference in the community.
    • Growth: Gain hands-on experience and develop valuable skills in PR, communication, coordination, and reporting.
    • Learning: Access training sessions and workshops to enhance your knowledge and skills.
    • Networking: Build relationships with industry professionals, NGOs, and media outlets.

    How to Apply

    If you are interested in joining us as an intern, please send your resume and a cover letter to anandavriddhifoundation@gmail.com. We look forward to welcoming passionate and dedicated individuals to our team.

    AVF is committed to equal opportunity and encourages applications from all qualified individuals. Join us and be a part of a team that is dedicated to creating a positive impact in society.
