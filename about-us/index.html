    Know About Us

    We are an NGO redefining your contribution to society!

    The Ananda Vriddhi Foundation is a non-profit organization that uses the latest technologies, such as Virtual Reality (VR), Augmented Reality (AR), and Mixed Reality (MR), to spread happiness and improve people's lives. Our goal is to make these technologies available to the under priveleged and needy ones in the society.

    Our Vision

    Our vision is to build a more inclusive society where the elderly and other needy ones can also enjoy the advantages of the digital transformation.

    Our Mission

    Our mission is to utilize latest technologies to improve educational opportunities for underprivileged children, bring joy to the elderly through specially designed virtual tours, offer engaging VR programs for patients to manage chronic pain, and deliver valuable virtual skill development experiences to diverse community groups.

    Our Supporters

    We extend our gratitude to the esteemed organizations for supporting us and relying on our vision and objectives to strive for a bigger and better cause.

    Our Journey

    Why we started.

    Our journey began with realizing the need to spread happiness, motivation, and positivity for the upliftment of society, especially for underserved sectors of society. With a strong desire to contribute our best, we started the Ananda Vriddhi Foundation and leveraged our technical expertise to bridge the digital gap and create new opportunities. By implementing advanced technologies, we help address the technological upgradation needed by the community and aspire to form a joyful and sustainable society.
