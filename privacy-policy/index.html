Privacy Policy

Updated on 13th April 2026

1. Introduction

Ananda Vriddhi Foundation ("we," "our," "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of all individuals ("you," "your") who visit our website www.anandavriddhifoundation.org ("Website") or interact with our services, including making donations, subscribing to updates, or applying for volunteer/internship opportunities.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, disclose, and safeguard your information. By accessing or using the Website, you consent to the practices described in this Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

2.1 Personal Information

Collected when you:

  • Make a donation
  • Create an account
  • Subscribe to newsletters
  • Contact us
  • Apply for volunteer, internship, or career opportunities

This may include:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Postal address
  • Payment information (processed securely by third-party gateways)
  • Date of birth (if required for volunteer/internship eligibility)
  • Any information voluntarily submitted through forms

2.2 Non-Personal Information

Automatically collected through cookies, analytics tools, and server logs:

  • Browser type
  • Device information
  • IP address
  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on the Website
  • Referring URLs
  • General usage patterns

This information helps us improve Website performance and user experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

By accepting this Privacy Policy, you expressly consent to the use of your information for:

  • Processing and managing donations
  • Issuing receipts and 80G certificates
  • Responding to queries and communication
  • Managing volunteer, internship, and career applications
  • Sending newsletters, updates, and program information
  • Improving Website functionality and user experience
  • Conducting analytics and performance monitoring
  • Complying with legal, regulatory, and audit requirements
  • Preventing fraud, misuse, or unauthorized access

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

4. Disclosure of Your Information

We may share your information with:

4.1 Service Providers

Trusted third-party vendors who assist with:

  • Payment processing
  • Email delivery
  • Cloud hosting
  • Data analytics
  • Website maintenance

These providers are obligated to protect your information.

4.2 Legal Compliance

We may disclose information:

  • When required by law
  • In response to court orders, government requests, or regulatory authorities
  • To protect our rights, safety, or property

4.3 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, restructuring, or transfer of assets, user information may be transferred as part of the organization's continuity.

5. Security of Your Information

We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your data, including:

  • Encrypted payment gateways
  • Secure servers
  • Access controls
  • Regular monitoring

However, no method of electronic transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we take all reasonable measures to protect your information.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Improve Website performance
  • Personalize user experience
  • Analyze traffic and usage patterns

You may disable cookies through your browser settings. However, some Website features may not function properly without them.

7. Your Data Protection Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Restrict processing under certain conditions
  • Object to processing
  • Withdraw consent for communications

To exercise these rights, email us at avfoundation24@outlook.com.

8. Consent

By using the Website, submitting information, or making a donation, you consent to:

  • Collection and processing of your data
  • Communication via email, SMS, or phone
  • Use of cookies and analytics tools

You may withdraw consent for non-essential communication at any time.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Website is not intended for children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we discover such data, we will delete it promptly.

10. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary for:

  • Legal compliance
  • Donation and audit requirements
  • Operational needs
  • Volunteer/internship documentation

After the retention period, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

11. Links to Third-Party Websites

Our Website may contain links to:

  • Facebook

  • LinkedIn
  • Other external platforms

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites. Users should review their respective privacy policies.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the Website constitutes acceptance of the revised Policy.
