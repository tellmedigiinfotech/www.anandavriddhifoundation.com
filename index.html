    What we do

    Ananda Vriddhi Foundation works in the following areas

    At Ananda Vriddhi Foundation, we strive to bring joy to the Sr.Citizens and educate the needy sections of the society using VR technology. By bridging digital gaps, we provide impactful solutions that make learning a fun experience and promote essential skill upgrades. Addressing the boredom challenge faced by Sr.Citizens we take them on a Virtual and truly immersive journey from the comfort of their home thus supporting health management .
