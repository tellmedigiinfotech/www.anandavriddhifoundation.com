Who We Are

Bringing Joy Through Technology

Ananda Vriddhi Foundation is a pioneering NGO in India using high-quality VR footage to give virtual tours of India's most beautiful destinations to underserved communities. In collaboration with TellMe Digiinfotech, we procure immersive content and conduct our signature "Dekho Apna Desh" programme.

Innovation with Purpose

We harness cutting-edge VR technology not only for entertainment, but also for genuine social impact.

Inclusive Outreach

Serving old age homes, orphanages, and schools for economically weaker and differently-abled communities.

Fully Compliant

We hold all necessary regulatory approvals to conduct activities and receive donations transparently.We issue receipt for claiming IT exemption under Sec 80G.

What We Do

Three Pillars of Compassion

Our social outreach spans three vital segments, each united by one goal — spreading joy and knowledge through immersive virtual experiences.

Elderly Care & Well-Being

Old Age Homes

We bring immersive VR travel experiences to senior citizens, helping combat loneliness and boredom. From the comfort of their homes, elders virtually visit the Taj Mahal, Kerala backwaters, Himalayan peaks and witness Aarti of Shirdi Sai Baba sparking joy and supporting cognitive health.

  • •Combats loneliness and social isolation
  • •Stimulates memory and cognitive well-being
  • •Virtual pilgrimage experiences across India

Dreams Without Boundaries

Orphanages

Children in orphanages rarely get the chance to travel. Through VR, we give them the gift of exploration — letting them discover India's heritage, wildlife & nature. Indulge in adventure of rafting etc. Every session brings wonder, laughter, and new dreams.

  • •Immersive VR Tours 360° high-quality footage of India's heritage sites, temples, hill stations, adventure and national landmarks.
  • •Encourages curiosity, creativity, and learning
  • •Joyful shared experiences in group settings

Education for All Abilities

Schools

We partner with schools serving economically weaker sections and differently-abled students. VR transforms learning from textbooks into experiences — making geography, history, and culture come alive in ways students never imagined.

  • •Gaining knowledge through immersive virtual tourist vistis
  • •Equal access to immersive education for EWS students
  • •Supports differently-abled learners with rich sensory experiences

Impact in Action

Our Signature Programme

🇮🇳 Dekho Apna Desh

India is breathtaking. Not everyone gets to see it. We change that.

In partnership with TellMe Digiinfotech, we procure high-quality VR footage of India's most iconic destinations and bring them to communities who may never get to travel. A virtual pilgrimage of joy, wonder, and national pride.

Immersive VR Tours

360° high-quality footage of India's heritage sites, temples, hill stations, and national landmarks.

Pan-India Coverage

From the Himalayas to Rameshwaram, from Rajasthan forts to Meghalaya — India comes alive.

Joy & Education

Every session combines entertainment with learning — geography, culture, and history through experience.
